Hi, I’m Dorina. A graphic designer, problem solver, and creative thinker, currently based in Denmark. I’m passionate about innovation, branding, and sustainability.

I grew up in Hungary where I had the privilege to study ceramics, academic drawing, and art history. I am influenced by my traditional arts and crafts background to this day. I believe designers can make an impact on society and create a better future for everyone. My educational background includes a Bachelor's Degree in Innovation and Entrepreneurship and an Academy Profession Degree in Design, Technology, and Business. The combination of my art, design and business background leads me to aesthetically pleasing designs driven by strategic thinking.
