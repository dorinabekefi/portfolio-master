About Me

I’m Dorina, a designer, storyteller, and creative strategist who thrives at the intersection of aesthetics and problem-solving. With a background in graphic design and business studies, I bring a unique blend of visual creativity and strategic thinking to every project I take on.

My work is about more than just making things look good—it’s about communicating ideas in a way that resonates. Whether it’s building a brand identity, crafting compelling visuals, or finding unexpected solutions, I believe design should be both beautiful and functional.

What sets me apart? I see the big picture while obsessing over the details. I approach every project with curiosity, adaptability, and a deep appreciation for storytelling. I believe in design that not only captures attention but also sparks connection.

Beyond design, I love bringing people together, whether it’s through collaborations, events, or creative projects. I find inspiration in the things that make life rich—art, music, movement, and the stories of those who dare to think differently.
