KaffeBraenderiet
Branding, Packaging

KaffeBrænderiet is a Copenhagen-based artisan coffee roastery dedicated to delivering freshly roasted, high-quality coffee. Their philosophy is simple: coffee tastes best when sourced in small batches and enjoyed at peak freshness.

Through our discovery process, we identified their unique selling proposition: “Always fresh, hand-roasted in Gentofte.” Our goal was to craft an identity that embodies these values.

The logomark takes inspiration from the sun symbol in the Gentofte crest, reinforcing a sense of heritage and craftsmanship. To highlight the freshness of each roast, we designed a unique date picker system for the packaging. The roast master personally marks the roast date and type by hand, turning this detail into a signature of craftsmanship.

This project was a collaboration with Sharmarke Hujale, a talented brand experience designer and writer. Explore his work and insights on his website: https://hujale.dk/ To order coffee go to: https://kaffebraenderiet.dk/
