Ecomil
Branding

Ecomil is company based in Spain. They are specialised in producing plant based diaryfree milk alternatives. All their products are certified organic, dairy-free, lactose-free, and without animal ingredients.

During my research I found out that Generation Z consumes more plant based milk than any generation ever before. That is why the goal was to rebrand Ecomil with Gen Z in mind.
