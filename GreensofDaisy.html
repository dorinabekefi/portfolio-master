Greens of Daisy
Branding, Packaging

Greens of Daisy is a Hungarian skincare brand founded by Bukovicsné Békefi Daisy, a horticultural engineer, mother of three, wife, and, above all, a woman who has always been intrigued by conscious and effective skincare. The brand's products are developed and manufactured in Hungary, offering natural cosmetics that are vegan, cruelty-free, paraben-free, SLS-free, and fragrance-free. Their product range includes skincare essentials such as facial creams, eye creams, serums, toners, lip balms, and hand creams, catering to those who appreciate natural ingredients. All products are free from ethyl alcohol, artificial colorants, petroleum derivatives, and palm oil. The unique formulations hold immense value, meeting the needs of individuals seeking gentle yet potent skincare solutions.

As the brand designer behind Greens of Daisy's visual identity, I had the privilege of capturing the essence of their ethos through design. From logo creation to packaging design, my work for Greens of Daisy embodies their commitment to purity, sustainability, and effective skincare. Through harmonious visuals and thoughtful branding, I've helped bring to life the brand's mission of providing gentle yet potent skincare solutions for individuals who value natural ingredients and conscious living.
